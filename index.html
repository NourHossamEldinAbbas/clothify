Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order in bulk?
Simply navigate to the 'Fabrics' or 'Wholesale' section, select your desired material and quantity, and proceed to checkout. For orders over 500 units, please contact our support for special rates.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Visa, MasterCard, Fawry, and Cash on Delivery (for orders under 5000 EGP). Secure online payments are processed via our banking partners.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we ship throughout Egypt. International shipping to select countries in the MENA region will be available starting next quarter.
